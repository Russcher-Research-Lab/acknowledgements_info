# Acknowledgements Info

A collection of ready-to-use acknowledgment statements for recognizing contributions from funding agencies and supporting organizations.

---

## 📌 Usage

Copy and paste the appropriate acknowledgment text directly into your manuscript, report, or presentation.  
Be sure to follow any additional guidelines required by your publisher or institution.

---

## 🏛️ SRNL–BRSA Acknowledgment

Use the following text when recognizing support from the Savannah River National Laboratory (SRNL) through the Battelle Savannah River Alliance (BRSA):

```text
This work was supported, in part, at Clemson University by the State of South Carolina through funding for the Battelle Savannah River Alliance Workforce Development Program.
```
